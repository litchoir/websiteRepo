Giving

  • music_noteWhy give to the Notre Dame Liturgical Choir?
    The Notre Dame Liturgical Choir has enriched the worship experience for countless congregations through its ministry at the Basilica of the Sacred Heart. Thanks to the generosity of our benefactors, the Liturgical Choir established an endowment fund to ensure the Choir's future success. We welcome contributions that support our mission of providing sacred choral music for the University community.
  • mail_outlineHow do I give?
    Gifts to the University may be directed to the Notre Dame Liturgical Choir endowment by contacting the University's Development Office.

    1. By Telephone: (574)-631-7164

    2. By Mail: Development Office
      1100 Grace Hall
      University of Notre Dame
      Notre Dame, IN 46556


    Please indicate your intent to donate to the Liturgical Choir on the check, pledge form, or by an accompanying letter or phone call.
  • laptopCan I give online?
    If you would like to give a gift online using a credit card, visit the Development Office's website. Please express your intent to designate your gift to the Liturgical Choir by indicating as such within the "Comments" section of the online form.
  • gradeDoes my gift count towards my participation in the Notre Dame football ticket lottery?
    Gifts to the Liturgical Choir do qualify toward fulfilling the minimum contribution limits for participation in the annual football ticket lottery, if received by December 31.
  • attach_moneyWhat can I do with my gift?
    Gifts to the Liturgical Choir are designated toward the Choir's endowment fund. This fund is part of the University endowment which is managed by the University's Investment Office. Gifts to this fund will ensure the future success of the choir's ministry.
  • publicWhat are the Liturgical Choir's needs?
    Our current partial list of needs includes:

    1. Contributions to the Liturgical Choir endowment.
    2. New robes for the choir
    3. Hymnals and music for choir members
    4. Support for the choir's annual retreat
